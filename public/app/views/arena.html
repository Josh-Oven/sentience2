Game Objective

As the sole survivor outrunning humanity's biggest threat - a black hole created through their own arrogance - you must evade any oncoming space debris and harvest whats left of human resources.

Abilities

Escaping the biggliest black hole ever known to man kind wont be easy, but you've got a few super advanced abilites to help you on your way.

Star Skip - ("SPACEBAR")

Hold any arrow key and press the spacebar button to execute a small skip through space.

Black Toll - (Keyboard "A")

Press "A" to launch a controled black hole into your enviroment. Best used as a temporary shield.

Lance of Light - (Keyboard "S")

Activating LoL will cause it to tear through time and space, ripping apart everything in its path.

Only Escape - (Keyboard "D")

Using Only Escape will give you a massive speed boost - causing you to outrun the black hole. Make sure you have sufficient fuel before activating.

Space Debris

Dodge space debris to avoid losing health. If your health bar is getting low, dont panic! health orbs will periodically spawn to help you heal.

Orbs

Gathering orbs is the most effective way to earn points and stay in the game.

Health Orb

Collecting one health orb will restore 10% of your health.

Fuel Orb

Collecting one fuel orb will increase your fuel supply by 5%.

Point Orb

Collecting one point orb will provide +10 points to your score.
